California State Library
Library Services and Technology Act (LSTA)
Fiscal Year 2001/2002

APPLICATION (LSTA 6)

With funding from the Library Services and Technology Act (LSTA), the UCSD Libraries, in collaboration with the San Diego Historical Society developed this site to make ocean related resources available to a wide range of California citizens. Kevin Starr, State Librarian of California announced the award for $213,161 in September 2001.
